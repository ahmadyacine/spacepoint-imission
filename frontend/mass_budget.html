📦

Understanding the Volume Columns

Vol/Unit (mm³) — The volume of a single component, calculated as: Length × Width × Height. This tells you how much space one piece occupies inside the CubeSat.

Total Vol (mm³) — The total space taken by all units of that component: Vol/Unit × Quantity. This is what actually contributes to the satellite's internal volume usage.

💡 The sum of all Total Vol values is compared against the Available Volume of the selected CubeSat size to determine if everything fits.
