⚡ How Power Budget Works

📐 Power (mW) = Voltage (V) × Current (mA)
⏱️ Energy/Orbit (mWh) = Power × Active Time ÷ 60
📅 Energy/Day (mWh) = Energy/Orbit × Orbits per Day
🔢 Required Solar Cells = ⌈Total Power (W) ÷ Power per Cell (W)⌉
☀️ Generated Power (W) = Selected Cells × Power per Cell
⚖️ Power Margin (W) = Generated Power − Total Consumption
